The view of the viewed

August 26th - September 3rd

ABOUT

PERSPECTIVA is a living exhibition that invites you to travel on the journey of a life model

Through two art installations and live performances you will have a taste of the view of the viewed

How is it to be observed? How is it to be described?

How do you perceive your persona, your image?

Special events

Drawing from life, live music and wine

Models, artists and musicians performing and freely experimenting together.

There are selected artists creating pieces during the event (invite only) however EVERYONE is welcome to bring their art materials and create within the space.
