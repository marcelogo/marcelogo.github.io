PERSPECTIVA

is a Living Exhibition that invites you to travel on the journey of a life model

Through two art installations and live performances you will have a taste of the view of the viewed

There are selected artists creating pieces during the event (invite only).

However EVERYONE is welcome to bring their art materials and create within the space.

How is it to be observed? How is it to be described?

How do you perceive your persona, your image?
